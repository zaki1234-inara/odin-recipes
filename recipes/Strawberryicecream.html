The Strawberry ice cream recipe

This strawberry ice cream is super easy to make. This recipe doesn't use an egg-custard base but still tastes rich and creamy.

This eggless strawberry ice cream recipe is rich, creamy, and full of fresh and fruity flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mashed fresh strawberries
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • Â¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 drops red food coloring (Optional)
    •

    Steps

    1. Gather all ingredients.
    2. Combine strawberries, milk, cream, sugar, vanilla, salt, and food coloring in a large bowl.
    3. Pour strawberry mixture into the freezer bowl of an ice cream maker; freeze according to the manufacturer's directions.
    4. Transfer to an airtight container and freeze until firm.
    5. Enjoy !!
